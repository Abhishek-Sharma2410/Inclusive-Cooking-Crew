All About ICC

We are a newly found service that aims to empower individuals by providing essential life skills for independent meal preparation. Leveraging their existing abilities and our supportive guidance, we enhance gross motor skills and social communication. Our program focuses on affordable, easy, and nutritious cooking. Additionally, we foster friendship building through shared mealtime experiences, allowing participants to enjoy the food prepared during our sessions together with their friends.
